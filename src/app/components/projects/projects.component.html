Mobile App Projects

MyDocs - Backup android app

An Android application designed to back up your media files, such as photos, videos, and contact details. You can also keep your files secure by adding them to a private section for extra privacy.

  • Technologies:
    • Android Studio (Java) and Firebase
  • Database Used:
    • Firebase
App Features:
  • End-to-End Encryption: All data is securely stored with end-to-end encryption, ensuring your media files and personal information are protected.
  • Easy Login: Quickly log in using your email ID for a seamless experience.
  • Responsive Media Playback: Enjoy smooth and responsive playback for videos and photos within the app.
MPlayer - Music Player

MPlayer is a simple, versatile, and user-friendly Music player android app built using React Native, a popular framework for developing cross-platform mobile applications.

  • Technologies:
    • JavaScript- React-Native
  • GitHub Link:
    • Music Player
App Features:
  • Local Music Library Access: Automatically scans and accesses all music files stored on your device, allowing you to play your favorite tracks directly from your personal collection.
  • Favorites: Easily add songs to your favorites list with a single tap, allowing you quick access to your most-loved tracks.
  • Background Play: Continue playing music while using other apps or when your screen is off.
